# VisionBuilt and CURA Website Specification

## Document Status

**Parent brand:** VisionBuilt  
**Product:** CURA  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Primary source of truth:** `CURA_PRODUCT_CONSTITUTION.md`

This document defines the initial VisionBuilt company website and CURA product marketing experience.

The first website should establish credibility, explain the product quickly, collect beta interest, and support event networking.

It is not the Mission Control dashboard.

When this document conflicts with the Product Constitution, the Constitution wins.

---

## 1. Website Purpose

The website must:

1. Establish VisionBuilt as a real product company.
2. Introduce CURA clearly.
3. Explain CURA within twenty seconds.
4. Demonstrate the capture-to-output transformation.
5. Show Learn, Create, and Work.
6. Show multimodal understanding.
7. Show Visual Briefs.
8. Explain privacy honestly.
9. Collect beta signups.
10. Provide contact and support.
11. Support QR-code sharing.
12. Present future VisionBuilt direction without overpromising.
13. Provide legal pages.
14. Prepare for future products.
15. Build trust before the app is publicly available.

---

## 19. FAQ Page

## What is CURA?

CURA is a mobile capture and knowledge activation app. It turns audio, video, photos, documents, and links into organized notes, content, visual briefs, and next actions.

## What does CURA stand for?

Capture. Understand. Refine. Activate.

## Is CURA only for creators?

No. CURA includes Learn, Create, and Work for everyday capture.

## Does CURA edit videos?

No. CURA may identify clips, timestamps, hooks, captions, and visual direction, but it is not a professional video editor.

## Does CURA post to social media?

Not in the first release. CURA can copy an output, open another app, or use the iOS Share Sheet. The user decides what gets published.

## Can CURA understand video visuals?

Yes. Full Video analysis may understand spoken audio, visible text, slides, screens, scenes, and demonstrated actions.

## Can CURA process YouTube links?

CURA is designed to analyze supported public YouTube links. Availability depends on access and provider support.

## Can CURA process Instagram or TikTok links?

Some public links may be supported. Platform restrictions may require users to upload a file they have permission to process.

## Does CURA store my recordings?

Originals stay on the user’s device by default. Smart Mode may temporarily upload content for processing and delete temporary source files under the stated retention policy.

## Does CURA train AI on my content?

The intended policy is no training on user recordings, files, transcripts, or notes by default. Final language will reflect verified production-provider terms.

## Can I export my information?

Yes. CURA is designed for user-owned exports through Files, iCloud Drive, and supported formats.

## Does CURA support Apple Shortcuts?

Apple Shortcuts are planned as a core product surface.

## When will CURA launch?

CURA is currently being developed as an iPhone beta. Join the beta list for updates.

## Is CURA free?

A limited free experience and a paid Pro plan are planned. Final pricing will be announced after beta testing.

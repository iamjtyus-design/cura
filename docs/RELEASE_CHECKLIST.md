# CURA Release Checklist

## Product

1. Constitution and Product documents match the build.
2. V1 boundaries remain intact.
3. Learn, Create, and Work work from the same Curated Note.
4. No unfinished dashboard or editor is exposed.
5. Unsupported features are labeled accurately.

## Engineering

1. Production build succeeds.
2. Unit tests pass.
3. UI tests pass.
4. CI passes.
5. No secrets committed.
6. Crash reporting is scrubbed.
7. Analytics is content-free.
8. Migrations tested.
9. Rollback plan documented.
10. Dependency licenses reviewed.

## Capture and processing

1. Recording recovery tested.
2. Large upload tested.
3. Offline queue tested.
4. Partial completion tested.
5. Provider outage tested.
6. Temporary deletion verified.
7. Transcript origin verified.
8. Multimodal mismatch tested.

## Privacy and legal

1. Privacy Policy reviewed.
2. Terms reviewed.
3. Recording notice reviewed.
4. Account deletion in app.
5. Session deletion works.
6. App Store privacy labels complete.
7. Vendor data terms verified.
8. No-training statement verified.
9. Support, privacy, and security contacts work.
10. Trademark review completed.
11. Insurance reviewed.

## Subscription

1. Product IDs correct.
2. Monthly plan works.
3. Annual plan works.
4. Trial works.
5. Restore works.
6. Server entitlement works.
7. Limit reached behavior works.
8. Expiration preserves local data.
9. Price copy is correct.
10. Terms and Privacy links open.

## App Store

1. App name verified.
2. Subtitle verified.
3. Screenshots truthful.
4. Description truthful.
5. Support URL works.
6. Privacy URL works.
7. Export compliance answered.
8. Account deletion documented.
9. Review notes explain recording and temporary processing.
10. Test account provided when needed.

## Website

1. VisionBuilt and CURA relationship is clear.
2. Waitlist works.
3. Claims match product status.
4. Legal pages work.
5. Mobile performance is acceptable.
6. Accessibility basics pass.
7. No fake testimonials or logos.
8. Event QR uses a stable URL.

## Final approval

1. Founder approval.
2. Engineering approval.
3. Privacy and legal approval.
4. Release version tagged.
5. Current State updated.
6. Known issues published internally.
